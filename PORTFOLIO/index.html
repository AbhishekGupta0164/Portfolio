About Me & My Work

I’m Abhishek Gupta, A student of B.tech CS 2nd year student & A passionate photographer and visual storyteller, Vlogger, capturing moments where technology, nature, and human emotion intersect.

My journey in photography began with curiosity — observing light, shadows, and patterns that often go unnoticed. Over time, this curiosity evolved into a creative pursuit focused on storytelling through images. Through street, nature, architecture, and experimental photography, I aim to freeze moments that feel alive, meaningful, and futuristic — aligning with Techfest IIT Bombay’s vision of innovation and creativity.

Exploring the intersection of technology, nature, culture, and light through photography. I capture innovative moments for Techfest's theme of "Future Visions."

Each image reflects my passion for visual storytelling and technical excellence.
